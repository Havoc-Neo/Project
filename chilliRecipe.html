Chilli

Yields: 6-8 servings

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
1 onion, chopped
>1 small green bell pepper, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
2(16 ounce) cans red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
>2(14 1/2 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1 teaspoon ground cumin

Directions

  1. Cook first 4 ingredients in a large skillet over medium high heat, stirring until beef crumbles and is no longer pink; drain. Place mixture in 5 1/4 slow cooker; stir in beans and remaining ingredients. Cook at high for 3-4 hrs or at low temp 5-6 hours.
  2. Notes: If you want to thicken the sauce, stir in finely crushed saltine crackers until the desired thickness is achieved.
